Chapter 1: Trigonometric Identities

Pythorean Identities

MATH(\sin^2\theta+\cos^2\theta = 1)
MATH(1 + \tan^2\theta = \sec^2\theta)
MATH(1 + \cot^2\theta = \csc^2\theta)

Sum and Difference

MATH(\sin(u \pm v) = \sin u \cos v \pm \cos u \sin v)
MATH(\cos(u \pm v) = \cos y \cos v \mp \sin y \sin v)
MATH(\tan(u \pm v) = \frac{\tan u \pm \tan v}{1 \mp \tan u \tan v})

Double Angle Identities

MATH(\sin(2\theta) = 2 \sin \theta \cos \theta)
MATH(\cos(2\theta) = \cos^2\theta - \sin^2\theta)
MATH(\tan(2\theta) = \frac{2\tan\theta}{1 - \tan^2\theta})
